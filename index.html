Multilingual AI & Knowledge Hub for Farmers

🚨 The Problem

90M+ Indian farmers face critical barriers
  • Language Barriers: Most apps are English-only, excluding rural farmers
  • Digital Divide: Low digital literacy limits technology adoption
  • Fragmented Information: No single platform for all farming needs
  • Poor Connectivity: Rural areas lack reliable internet access
  • Middleman Dependency: Farmers lose profits due to information asymmetry
Farmers lose 20-30% income due to lack of market information and proper guidance

Technical Architecture

Scalable Microservices + AI-Powered Backend

🏗️ Technology Stack

Frontend Flutter - Cross-platform mobile app with offline caching
Backend Microservices (MCP) architecture with Node.js + Express
AI Models HuggingFace (crop guidance) + Coqui AI (voice processing)
Database Firebase Firestore for real-time data + Cloud Storage
External APIs AgMarkNet (prices), IMD (weather), AgriCoop (schemes)
Hackathon MVP Focus:
✓ Voice AI Chatbot (crop guidance)
✓ Real-time mandi price lookup
✓ Basic community Q&A platform
